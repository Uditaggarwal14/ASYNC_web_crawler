Exams that are uploaded to the Platform are considered User material, please refer to the section section INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS AND USER MATERIAL for terms and conditions on User material. For each exam, you may enable and disable a range of tools in the exam settings, and you select what personal information the students have to enter before starting the exam. When you are finished editing the exam and its settings, you may let students access the exam and see the questions and/or the writing area in the Platform by distributing a so-called “exam key”. The students go to exam.net, enter the exam key along with their personal information as selected by you, and then gain access to the specific exam. During the exam, you can monitor the progress of the students’ work on the Platform. When the exam is finished, the students’ answers may be viewed by you. Students’ answers may also be printed and/or downloaded to a chosen space or folder of your choice and control, for sake of clarity, outside of the Platform.  Student personal information notice Exam.net has no other purpose to collect and process student personal information apart from ensuring that the teacher can connect submitted answers to the right student and to prevent cheating.
